\begin{frame}{Visibility of Updates and Latency Measurement}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item The Follower actively monitors the \texttt{replication\_log} and accurately captures operation IDs generated by the Leader.
        \item The measured network latency is explicitly calculated by comparing the generation time on the Leader and the precise reading time on the Follower node.
    \end{itemize}

% --- SLIDE 9: READ-ONLY EVIDENCE (Shifted from 8) ---
% Presenter: Follower
\begin{frame}{Architectural Security: "Read-Only" Enforcement}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textbf{Single-Leader Constraint:} To guarantee data integrity and eliminate split-brain issues, the Follower instance is constrained to operate strictly in Read-Only mode.
        \item As demonstrated, direct data manipulation attempts (e.g., \texttt{DELETE}) on the Follower are explicitly blocked and strictly rejected by the PostgreSQL engine.
    \end{itemize}
